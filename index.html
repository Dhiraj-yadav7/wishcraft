Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about time capsules, media formats, or sharing? We have answers.

How does the Digital Time Capsule work? ▼
When creating a surprise card, you can toggle the "Digital Time Capsule" lock and set an unlock date/time. Before this date, any visitor visiting the link will only see a countdown clock and title. All images, timelines, videos, and guestbook entries are redacted/hidden until the clock hits zero.
Can I upload my own background music? ▼
Yes! WishCraft has built-in high-quality ambient soundtracks (romantic chimes, piano, birthday synth). Premium accounts can also embed Spotify playlists/songs directly into a mini Spotify-style player.
How do people sign the guestbook? ▼
Any visitor visiting the shared link can click the floating "Sign Guestbook" button on the bottom right. A modern panel slides out where they can type their name, write a message, choose a reaction emoji, and submit it. The list updates in realtime.
Can I map my own domain to the page? ▼
Yes, our Studio Plan supports custom domain mapping. You can configure CNAME/A records in your DNS dashboard to point your surprise link to a custom domain (e.g. `surprise.anshika.com`).
